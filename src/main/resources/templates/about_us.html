Platform overview

What this system does

This application keeps the student workflow in one place: registration, batch assignment, batch and year setup, student payment status, and marks entry. The goal is to keep the admin screens readable and the student data tied to the right cohort.

The admin dashboard is used to create batches and years, then manage student payment status for a selected month. The marks page filters the correct students for the selected batch, year, month, and week, while the student dashboard shows the stored weekly marks in a compact view.

Use the top menu to move between the public pages and the two dashboards.

Key areas

Registration Students are linked to a batch before account creation completes.
Batch control Batches can share a year when the place is different.
Marks Weekly marks are saved against a selected batch, year, month, and week.
Access Students and admins use different dashboards after login.
